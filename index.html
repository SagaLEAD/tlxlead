Why TLX Exists

The Me2We high fades.
Your growth shouldn't.

Every conference ends. The conversations that felt transformative get buried under the weight of Monday morning. The LEAD community is full of extraordinary people — but extraordinary people rarely become your thinking partners by accident.

TLX exists to change that. A structured leadership community built for LEAD alumni and current participants — designed to extend the energy of Me2We into sustained relationships, peer problem-solving, and real leadership conversations.

This is not another alumni network. This is a deliberate exchange.

Join the Founding Cohort

The Energy Dissipates

Without structure, the momentum from Me2We evaporates. TLX gives it somewhere to go.

Networks Stay Surface-Level

LinkedIn connections aren't relationships. Extraordinary people don't become thinking partners by accident — they need a structure to meet in.

Growth Needs Accountability

Leadership development is a practice, not an event. The leaders who grow most have someone who challenges them consistently, over time.

Experience Should Flow Both Ways

Alumni have hard-won insight. Current participants bring fresh perspective. TLX creates the conditions for that exchange to happen.

Program 01
TLX Mentorship

A structured leadership
development experience.

A guided mentorship program where experienced professionals support emerging leaders through career guidance, strategic thinking, and professional growth — with real structure and real accountability.

Every pair is hand-matched. Every cohort is intentional. The founding cohort launches from Me2We 2026 with 20 matched pairs.

🎯

1:1 Matched Pairs

Hand-matched based on industry, challenge, and what you want to give and receive. No algorithm.

📅

30 Min Monthly — Your Pair

One call per month. You set the agenda. Rotating: one brings a challenge, one brings perspective.

👥

45 Min Monthly — Cohort Call

All pairs together. A structured 45 minutes with a theme, a spotlight, and collective momentum.

⏱

6-Month Commitment

Then you decide — continue, rematch, or step back. No obligation. Just leadership.

How It Works
01

Apply & Get Matched

Short intake form — your cohort, what you want to give, what you want to receive. Every pair matched by hand within two weeks of Me2We.

02

Meet Your Pair

A guided first call with a simple framework. We set the foundation so you can focus on the work.

03

Monthly 1:1 Calls

30 minutes, once a month. You lead. One challenge, one perspective, one shift at a time.

04

Monthly Cohort Call

45 minutes with all founding pairs. Themes, spotlights, shared insight.

05

Six Months — Then You Decide

Continue with your pair, rematch with someone new, or take a break. You're always in control.

The Founder

Built by someone who has
done this work.

TLX was founded by Saglara Meyer — Senior HR Business Partner, certified executive coach (UC Berkeley ECI), and Co-Chair of the Mentorship Program at Lake Washington Human Resources Association, where she already leads mentor matching and program design for HR professionals.

As a current Stanford LEAD participant, Saglara experienced first-hand the gap between the energy of Me2We and the months that follow. TLX is her answer to that gap — built from real experience designing mentorship programs, not from theory.

Her background spans aerospace HR leadership at Triumph Group and Safran, cross-sector experience in the U.S. and Denmark, and a deep commitment to helping leaders grow with clarity, confidence, and strategy.
